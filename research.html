Research

Research Interests

My research curiosity centers on how RNA regulation and post-translational modifications shape cellular signaling pathways. I am particularly interested in understanding how subtle molecular changes can generate significant biological consequences.

Research Highlights

RNA-Mediated Regulatory Mechanisms

Exploring how non-coding RNA elements modulate protein modification systems and influence signaling outcomes.

Protein Modification & Signal Integration

Investigating the role of dynamic protein modifications in cellular response coordination and stability.

Experimental Reproducibility & Discipline

Building rigorous laboratory practices through systematic optimization of qPCR, Western blot, and molecular assays.

Technical Skills

qPCR · Western blot · SDS-PAGE · RNA extraction · Cell culture · Experimental troubleshooting · Data interpretation
